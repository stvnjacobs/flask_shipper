Status: experimental side project

About

This site aims to be a user interface for the EasyPost shipping api. A simple way to interact with many shippers at once could be beneficial. The main goal is simplicity for the user.

Roadmap & Ideas:

  • Weights & measures option/conversion
  • User accounts (high priority)
  • EasyPost signup workflow
  • Purchase and print shipping labels
  • Tracking packages
  • Warnings, concerns, can't ship away sister or cat, etc.
  • Launch on heroku, digital ocean, etc. button

Notes

  • USPS Rates Provided. These are Commercial Plus Pricing provided by easypost.
  • UPS Rates Provided (kind of) These are negotiated rates, equal to those from a newly created UPS account, since thats what they are.
